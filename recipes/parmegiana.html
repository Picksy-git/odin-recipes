Parmegiana

Description

This is an easy chicken Parm recipe that's delicious. You can do this in an over or a toaster oven.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray.
  • 1 package skinless, boneless chicken breasts.
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese.
  • 2 cups tomato sauce, or to taste.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Spray a baking pan with cooking spray. Place chicken on the prepared pan.
  2. Bake in the preheat oven for 20min, flipping chicken after 12min. Remove from the oven, add desire amount of sauce, and top each breast with mozarella.
  3. Bake until cheese is melted and chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 12 more min. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees.
