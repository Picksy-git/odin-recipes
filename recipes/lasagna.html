Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below to do the best lasagna ever.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce and tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spice and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the cheese mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to your taste.
  5. Cover with foil for the first 25min.
  6. Cook uncovered for the final 25min.
  7. Let the lasagna rest before serving
